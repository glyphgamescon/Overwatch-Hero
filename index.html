About Genji

Genji is a video game character that is playable in a new(ish) game called "Overwatch".
He is a robo-ninja with a knockout-awesome dragonblade,
glowing green, super-sharp shurikens that travel at an
amazing pace around the Overwatch battle field.

Overwatch is a team-based video game which involves the ability to aim well. In this game you chose from one of many characters to play as, like Genji. Each character has it's "ups" and "donws", so no character is better than any other. Overwatch is played online with 6v6 teams battling and capturing checkpoints to progress in the match.
